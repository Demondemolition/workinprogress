Suicide rates through time

The Data

  • Age-standardization means that all countries have the same distribution of ages


  • This weighting was set by the World Health Organization


  • Different country = different age distributions


  • This is important because different ages have different rates of suicide

Results

  • Suicide occurs amongst poor and rich countries


  • Very wealthy countries are less affected


  • Even countries with good social support networks (judged by WHO) are deeply effected
